Welcome To Aarya hotel & spa

Exceptional Hospitality and
Unmatched Relaxation

Welcome to Aarya Hotel & Spa Pvt. Ltd., a heritage hotel located in the vibrant heart of Thamel, Kathmandu. Our establishment beautifully blends traditional charm with modern comfort, creating a unique sanctuary for travelers seeking an authentic experience. With 72 elegantly appointed guest rooms, we cater to the diverse needs of our guests, ensuring a memorable stay.

At Aarya Hotel & Spa, we pride ourselves on offering genuine Nepalese hospitality, welcoming you with warm smiles and attentive service. Our luxurious spa facilities are designed to rejuvenate your senses, providing the perfect escape after a day of exploring the city.

Experience the rich culture and history of Kathmandu right outside your door, with easy access to the city's top attractions. For inquiries or reservations, feel free to reach out at info@aaryahotel.com or call us at 9851364938. Discover your home away from home at Aarya Hotel & Spa, where every visit is an unforgettable journey.
